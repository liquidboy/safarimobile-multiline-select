Demo's of different Select's converted to UL's

The left list is a "SELECT". The right list is the converted "UL"

This jquery plugin converts a SELECT into a UL and keeps the existing SELECT but hides it

important: SELECT supports multiple-select via ctrl+click ... On the UL for touch devices we cannot handle a "ctrl" so we are hijacking click to toggle selection (i.e. click to select, 2nd click to unselect)
